# About FunHaus Decor

FunHaus Decor is a home decor and lifestyle resource focused on bold, maximalist design — the kind of interiors that refuse to be beige.

## Why we exist

Most home decor content rewards safety. Neutral palettes. Predictable arrangements. Forgettable rooms.

We don't.

We cover the corners of interior design where personality wins: striped ceilings, color-blocked walls, vintage maximalism, circus-inspired geometry, the surreal-domestic aesthetic that defines our era's most interesting homes. Our content is for the homeowner, renter, or designer who's tired of beige.

## What we publish

- In-depth design guides with specific product recommendations
- Step-by-step painting and DIY tutorials
- Trend analysis grounded in real research
- Curated product roundups for the maximalist aesthetic

Every article is researched, written, and edited with care. We rely on aggregated reviews, manufacturer specifications, and design community knowledge — never fabricated experiences.

## How we make money

We participate in the Amazon Associates program and other affiliate networks. When you click through to a retailer and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This funds our content production. See our [Affiliate Disclosure](/disclosure) for details.

We do not accept payment for positive reviews. We do not run sponsored content disguised as editorial.
